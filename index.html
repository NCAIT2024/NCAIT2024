A purely peer-to-peer version of electronic cash would allow online payments to be sent directly from one party to another without going through a financial institution.Digital signatures provide part of the solution, but the main benefits are lost if a trusted third party is still required to prevent double-spending.

The network timestamps transactions by hashing them into an ongoing chain of hash-based proof-of-work, forming a record that cannot be changed without redoing the proof-of-work.

About the College and department

Atria Institute of Technology (AIT) in Bengaluru stands out for its industry-specific courses tailored to meet the dynamic market demands of the decade. The institution fosters a culture of comprehensive development, ensuring students are well-prepared for excellent placement opportunities through rigorous training that includes personality development and company-specific skills. AIT's commitment to student enrichment is evident in its diverse achievements, from the Rotaract Club's multiple awards to the impressive sportsmanship displayed in intercollegiate tournaments. The institute also prioritizes mental health with initiatives like Brave Together and maintains a vibrant educational ecosystem through regular alumni-industry interactions. A testament to its excellence, AIT has been awarded the prestigious NAAC A++ accreditation, celebrating the collective efforts of its community.

The MCA program at AIT plays an all-encompassing role in applying technological advances across various disciplines in engineering, sciences, and humanities. AIT established the MCA department under its umbrella in 2022. The course is affiliated with Visvesvaraya Technological University (VTU), Belagavi.
